Fretboard Master

Memorize fretboard notes the fun way!

The Fretboard Master is a tool designed to help you memorize fret note names in an easy and fun way!

How to play the game? It’s easy!

When the note is displayed find and click it on the fretboard

Be quick you - have only 15 seconds!

Good luck!
